<h1>Cappuccino</h1>
<p>Cappuccino is mild and creamy at the first sip from the generous ratio of milk to coffee. It is known to be the best method for bringing out the wonderful taste and aroma of coffee. The cappuccino carries a mild and creamy taste. Cappuccinos look very similar to the robes of the Capuchin Monks. That's where the name comes from. The origin can be traced back to Vienna. Cappuccino is best paired with scones.</p>
